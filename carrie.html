egg curry recipe

Ingredients

  • 4 to 5 boiled eggs
  • 2 tablespoons oil
  • 2 onions
  • 3 tomatoes
  • 1 green chilli
  • 1 teaspoon GINGER GARLIC PASTE
  • salt as needed
  • 1 cup of water as needed
  • 1 tablespoons Coriander leaves
  • 1 1/4 Kasuri methi
  • 1 teaspoon sugar
  • 4 tablespoons cream

preparation for egg curry

  1. Heat 1 tbsp oil in a nonstick pan and saute the eggs until golden.

  2. Remove the eggs to a plate and sprinkle 2 to 3 pinches of chilli powder.

how to make egg curry

  1. Add another tbsp oil.Saute cumin,bay leaf,cardamous and cloves just for a minute.

  2. next add and fry the onion and chilli until golden.

  3. add gimger garlic paste and saute until the raw smells goes off.

  4. then add tomatoes and saute well until the mixture turns mushy.

  5. next add red chilli powder,garam masala,coriander powder,tumeric,sugar and salt.

  6. fry everything well until a nice aroma comes out and the mixture leaves the sides of the pan.

  7. pour water just as needed to make gravy.

  8. cook until the curry thckness and oil begins seperate.

  9. check the salt and adjust if needed.

  10. add the boiled and fried eggs,kasuri methi if using.
